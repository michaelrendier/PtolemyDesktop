Definition

Minkowski space ℝ1,3 is ℝ4 with the indefinite inner product:

⟨u, v⟩ = −u0v0 + u1v1 + u2v2 + u3v3// Not positive-definite: ⟨u,u⟩ can be negative, zero, or positive4-vectors: xμ = (ct, x, y, z)
Geometric Intuition — What It Does

Minkowski space is the stage of special relativity. Events are points. 4-vectors are arrows between events. The inner product is Lorentz-invariant — every observer agrees on ⟨u,v⟩.

4-momentum: pμ = (E/c, p⃗). Mass-energy: pμpμ = −m²c² = invariant. Massless: pμpμ = 0.

Relational Analysis — Density · Pull · Attraction

Invariant density: ⟨p,p⟩ = −m²c² is the same for every observer — the mass is the Lorentz-invariant content. Boosting concentrates energy in one direction but the invariant mass is the irreducible density of the particle. In field theory: the mass term in the Lagrangian is the scalar density that can't be booted away.

Three Levels of Understanding
NOVICE High School · Events and Arrows
▶

In Minkowski space, a point is an event (a place and a time). An arrow between two events is a 4-vector (Δt, Δx, Δy, Δz). The Minkowski inner product of this arrow with itself tells you: negative = more time than space (timelike, causal), zero = light-like, positive = more space than time (spacelike, no causal connection).

JOURNEYMAN Collegiate · 4-Vector Algebra
▶

4-velocity: Uμ = γ(c, v⃗), with UμUμ = −c². 4-force: Fμ = dpμ/dτ. 4-current: Jμ = (cρ, J⃗). Conservation: ∂μJμ = 0. Every conservation law is a 4-divergence condition in Minkowski space.

MASTER Graduate / PhD · Wigner's Classification
▶

Wigner classified irreducible unitary representations of the Poincaré group (Minkowski symmetries: Lorentz + translations). They're labeled by (mass, spin): massive (m>0) or massless (m=0, labeled by helicity). Every elementary particle corresponds to one Wigner class. Minkowski space geometry = particle taxonomy.

Common Mistakes
MISTAKE 01
Confusing Minkowski space (flat, a fixed arena) with Minkowski spacetime (the physical model for special relativity). They're the same mathematical object.
MISTAKE 02
Using Euclidean dot product instead of Minkowski. Always use η.
MISTAKE 03
Forgetting that c=1 in natural units — make your unit choice explicit.
