Definition

The Clifford algebra Cl(V,Q) over a vector space V with quadratic form Q is generated by V subject to:

v² = Q(v)·1 for all v∈V// Encodes metric geometry in the algebra itselfdim Cl(V,Q) = 2^(dim V) (basis: all antisymmetric products of basis vectors)Non-commutative: vw ≠ wv in general
Geometric Intuition

The Clifford algebra unifies dot product and cross product into one geometric product: vw = v·w + v∧w. The scalar part is the metric; the bivector part encodes rotation. This is the algebra that rotations, reflections, and spinors all naturally live in.

Relational Analysis — Density · Pull · Attraction

Density of geometric information: every grade-k subspace of Cl(V,Q) encodes k-dimensional oriented volumes. Grade 0 = scalars (no extent), grade 1 = vectors (length), grade 2 = bivectors (area), up to grade n = pseudoscalar (volume). The full algebra = all possible geometric densities simultaneously.

Three Levels
NOVICEHigh School · The Algebra That Knows Geometry
▶

In ordinary algebra, numbers commute. Clifford algebra builds in the geometry: two perpendicular vectors anticommute (vw = −wv), two parallel vectors multiply to a scalar (v² = Q(v)). The result is an algebra where every element has geometric meaning — scalars, vectors, planes, volumes, and rotors.

JOURNEYMANCollegiate · Spinors as Minimal Left Ideals
▶

A spinor is an element of a minimal left ideal of Cl(n). For Cl(3,0): spinors are 2-component objects that transform under SU(2) ≅ Spin(3). A 2π rotation gives −ψ, not +ψ — the double-cover property comes from the algebra automatically, no extra structure needed.

MASTERGraduate · Periodicity Theorem
▶

Cl(n+8,0) ≅ Cl(n,0) ⊗ M₁₆(ℝ) — Bott periodicity mod 8. This is why supersymmetry has period 8 in spacetime dimension. The full classification: Cl(p,q) ≅ Mₙ(𝔽) where 𝔽 ∈ {ℝ,ℂ,ℍ} depends on (p−q) mod 8. Every division algebra appears exactly in this cycle.

Common Mistakes
MISTAKE 01
Confusing Clifford algebra with exterior (Grassmann) algebra — Clifford has a metric (v²=Q(v)), Grassmann does not (v²=0).
MISTAKE 02
Assuming all Clifford algebras are division algebras. Only Cl(0,0)≅ℝ, Cl(0,1)≅ℂ, Cl(0,2)≅ℍ are. Higher ones have zero divisors.
MISTAKE 03
Forgetting that the grade decomposition is not a subalgebra — products mix grades.
