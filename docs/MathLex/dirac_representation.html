Dirac Representation

The standard gamma matrix basis — block diagonal form separating large and small components
DEFINITION

The Dirac representation is a specific choice of gamma matrices where the Dirac equation separates cleanly into large (upper) and small (lower) spinor components in the non-relativistic limit.

DIRAC GAMMA MATRICES
γ⁰ = [[I, 0], [0, −I]]
γⁱ = [[0, σⁱ], [−σⁱ, 0]]
where σⁱ are Pauli matrices
