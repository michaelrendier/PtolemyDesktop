Definition

The Lorentz transform maps coordinates between inertial frames moving at relative velocity v:

t' = γ(t − vx/c²) x' = γ(x − vt) γ := 1/√(1−v²/c²) (Lorentz factor) singularity at v → c: γ → ∞
Geometric Intuition — What It Does

Euclidean rotation mixes x and y: x'=x·cosθ − y·sinθ. Lorentz transform mixes x and ct — but with hyperbolic trig instead of circular. The "angle" is rapidity φ = tanh⁻¹(v/c).

The light cone stays fixed — that's the invariant. Everything else rotates around it. Density in spacetime (events per volume) transforms inversely to the Lorentz factor.

Relational Analysis — Density · Pull · Attraction

Attraction/pull: the closer two events are to each other's light cones, the more a boost can make them simultaneous — or causally ordered. Causal structure is the geometry of pull in spacetime.

The invariant interval ds² = −c²dt² + dx² is the density-preserving quantity — it measures proper distance/time, which every observer agrees on.

Three Levels of Understanding
NOVICE High School · Moving Clocks Run Slow
▶

If you're moving fast, time ticks slower for you compared to someone standing still. This isn't an illusion — it's what the Lorentz transform says. At v = 0.87c, γ ≈ 2: your clock ticks half as fast.

Light always travels at c regardless of how fast you're moving. The Lorentz transform is what makes that possible without contradiction.

JOURNEYMAN Collegiate · Hyperbolic Rotation
▶

Define rapidity φ by tanh(φ) = v/c. Then γ = cosh(φ), γv/c = sinh(φ). The transform becomes:

[ct', x'] = [[cosh φ, −sinh φ],[−sinh φ, cosh φ]] [ct, x]

Rapidities add: φ₁₂ = φ₁ + φ₂. Velocities don't — they follow the relativistic addition formula. Rapidity is the natural variable.

MASTER Graduate / PhD · Lie Group SO(1,3)
▶

The Lorentz group O(1,3) is the group of linear maps preserving ημνxμxν = −c²t² + x² + y² + z². Its connected component SO⁺(1,3) has Lie algebra with generators Ji (rotations) and Ki (boosts) satisfying:

[Ji, Jj] = iεijkJk[Ki, Kj] = −iεijkJk (non-compact)

The non-compactness means unitary reps are infinite-dimensional — hence the need for quantum field theory.

Common Mistakes
MISTAKE 01
Using v > c in the formula — γ becomes imaginary. Nothing with mass reaches c.
MISTAKE 02
Adding velocities directly instead of using the relativistic formula: u' = (u−v)/(1−uv/c²).
MISTAKE 03
Confusing time dilation (one clock) with the full transform (two events at different positions).
